Learn more about interplanetary time zones

This is information of the Mercury,Venus,Earth and Mars Time zones.

There are four planets in the solar system that have crusts and feet. Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars all rotate on their own. The time zone exists. However, Venus and Mercury have a rotational speed that is fast and fast. There is no difference in proportion, so this site does not cover Venus and Mercury's time zones.

Mars has a rotation cycle that is very similar to Earth. One day is about 30 minutes longer than the Earth, and the season is also three months longer than the Earth. Thanks to this, the time zone on Mars is similar to Earth. The day humans settle on Mars, they will use this time zone.
